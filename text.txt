Here are the most solid, professional strategies to boost both traffic (viewership) and advertiser sign-ups:

### 1. Viewership Multipliers (Driving Traffic to the Canvas)
*   **The "Live Billboard" Embed Widget:** Create a small HTML snippet that anyone can embed on their own website/blog. It shows a live, mini-version of the canvas. You could offer free pixels to high-traffic blogs in exchange for them embedding the canvas on their site. This creates a massive web of backlinks and drives thousands of external eyeballs to your advertisers.
*   **"Pixel of the Day" Automated Newsletter:** Launch a Substack or weekly email round-up. Every week, feature 3 interesting startups or creators who bought a pixel. Advertisers will suddenly buy pixels *just* for the chance to get featured in your email blast and social media.
*   **Public Canvas API:** Expose a read-only API of the canvas. Developers love playing with data. If you let them query the canvas to build heatmaps or "canvas history" bots, they will share their projects on Hacker News and Reddit, driving massive organic nerd-cred and traffic to your site.

### 2. Advertiser Boosting (Getting More Businesses to Buy)
*   **"Verified Advertiser" Directory Pages:** Instead of *just* getting a square on the grid, every purchase also generates a dedicated, SEO-optimized profile page on your site (e.g., `buyaspot.in/advertisers/their-startup`). This turns BuyASpot into a high-quality Startup Directory. Businesses will gladly pay ₹500 just for the SEO backlink on that dedicated profile page alone.
*   **Industry-Specific "Neighborhoods":** Dedicate specific sections of the grid to specific industries. For example, announce: *"The top-right 10x10 block is now the AI Startup Neighborhood."* This creates intense FOMO. If an AI founder sees 5 of their competitors in that block, they will buy the remaining pixels immediately so they don't look left out.
*   **Wholesale / Agency Accounts:** Create a feature where marketing agencies can buy a massive block (e.g., 500 pixels at a discount) and sub-divide it to upload different ads for their various clients. This attracts buyers who will spend ₹25,000+ at once instead of just ₹99.
